## Who We Are

SynthAI is an independent AI tools review blog. We test, compare, and review AI tools with one goal: **helping you find the tools that actually work — and skip the ones that don't.**

## Our Promise

Every review on this site is based on **real-world testing**, not press releases or marketing materials. We buy or subscribe to every tool we review. We use them for real projects. We show real screenshots.

We will never rank a tool higher because they pay us more. Our editorial integrity is non-negotiable — it's the only reason readers trust us.

## How We Make Money

Transparency matters. Here's how SynthAI generates revenue:

1. **Affiliate commissions** — When you click a link and purchase a tool, we may earn a commission at no extra cost to you
2. **Display advertising** — We run non-intrusive ads through premium ad networks
3. **Sponsored content** — Clearly marked, and our opinions remain our own
4. **Digital products** — Guides, templates, and resources we create

We **never** let monetization influence our rankings or recommendations.
