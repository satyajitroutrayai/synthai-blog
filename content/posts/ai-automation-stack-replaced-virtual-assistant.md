This is not a story about AI replacing humans. It's a story about a solopreneur who was paying $1,500/month for a virtual assistant, realized most of the work was repetitive, and found a better way.

Here's my exact automation setup that handles 80% of what my VA used to do.

## What My VA Was Doing

Before I made the switch, my VA handled:

- Scheduling social media posts (5 hours/week)
- Drafting email newsletters (3 hours/week)
- Responding to routine emails (4 hours/week)
- Creating blog post outlines from my notes (3 hours/week)
- Basic image creation for blog posts (2 hours/week)
- Research for upcoming articles (3 hours/week)

Total: about 20 hours/week at $18/hour = $1,440/month.

## The 4-Tool AI Stack

### Tool 1: Claude Pro — $20/month
**Replaces:** Blog outlines, email drafts, research

I feed Claude my rough notes and voice memos (transcribed by Otter.ai) and it produces structured blog outlines, draft newsletters, and research summaries. I review and edit everything — this is not set-and-forget.

**Time saved:** ~9 hours/week

### Tool 2: Buffer + AI — $36/month
**Replaces:** Social media scheduling

Buffer's AI assistant generates social post variations from my blog content. I batch-approve a week's worth of posts every Sunday. The AI adapts content for each platform automatically.

**Time saved:** ~5 hours/week

### Tool 3: Canva Pro — $13/month
**Replaces:** Image creation

Canva's AI generates blog thumbnails and social graphics from templates I've set up. I have branded templates that the AI fills in — consistency without the effort.

**Time saved:** ~2 hours/week

### Tool 4: Zapier + ChatGPT — $28/month
**Replaces:** Routine email responses

I set up Zapier automations that draft responses to common email types (sponsorship inquiries, reader questions, partnership requests). The drafts go to my inbox for review before sending.

**Time saved:** ~4 hours/week

## The Math

| | Before (VA) | After (AI Stack) |
|---|---|---|
| Monthly Cost | $1,440 | $97 |
| My Review Time | 0 hrs/week | 3 hrs/week |
| Quality | Good | Good (with review) |
| Savings | — | $1,343/month |

## What I Still Do Myself

AI can't replace everything. I still personally handle:

- Final editing of all content (my voice matters)
- Strategy and planning
- Relationship building with sponsors
- Complex or sensitive email responses
- Recording videos and podcasts

## Important Caveats

This setup works because my tasks were largely repetitive and template-based. If your VA does creative strategy, relationship management, or complex problem-solving, AI tools won't replace them.

Also: I review everything before it goes out. "Automation" doesn't mean "zero oversight." The AI drafts, I approve.
