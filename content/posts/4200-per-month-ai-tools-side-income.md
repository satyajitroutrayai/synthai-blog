Let me be upfront: I'm not selling a course. I'm not going to pitch you a "system." This is just a transparent breakdown of how I used AI tools to build a side income stream that now generates around $4,200 per month.

It took 8 months to get here. The first two months, I made almost nothing. Here's everything I'd tell myself if I were starting over.

## The 5-Tool Stack

Here's exactly what I use and what I pay:

1. **Claude Pro** ($20/mo) — Content writing and research
2. **Surfer SEO** ($89/mo) — Keyword research and content optimization
3. **Canva Pro** ($13/mo) — Graphics for blog and social
4. **ConvertKit** (Free tier) — Email newsletter management
5. **Cloudflare Pages** ($0) — Blog hosting

**Total monthly cost: $122/mo**

## Revenue Breakdown (Month 8)

Here's where the money actually comes from:

- **Affiliate commissions:** $2,400/mo (mainly from AI tool reviews)
- **Display ads (Mediavine):** $1,100/mo
- **Digital product sales:** $500/mo (prompt template pack)
- **Sponsored posts:** $200/mo (one post)

**Total: ~$4,200/mo**

## What Actually Worked

The biggest game-changer was **comparison articles**. Posts like "Tool A vs Tool B" convert 3-5x better than general reviews because the reader is already in buying mode.

I use Claude to help me outline articles and draft sections, but I always add my real screenshots, honest opinions, and personal testing. Google can tell the difference between pure AI content and content with genuine experience.

## What I'd Do Differently

1. I'd start the email newsletter from Day 1 (I waited until Month 3)
2. I'd focus on fewer, higher-quality articles instead of trying to publish daily
3. I'd start with the highest-commission affiliate programs first
